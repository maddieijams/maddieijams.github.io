About



I was born in the midwest and have lived in Indianapolis most of my life. All throughout school, I was a committed distance runner, participating in cross country and track every year. Since graduating high school, I have spent most of my working life gaining experience cooking in different restaurants. During that time, I was diligently working on my film photogrpahy, eventually building my own darkroom in the closet of my apartment and going on to display and sell my black and white photographs at multiple venues. I was looking for more meaning in my work, which is how I ended up as the head chef for the Patachou Foundation, a nonprofit serving kids healthy meals. I loved my job but I was ready for a change. I wanted to challenge myself and learn something new, which is what led me to enroll in the JavaScript bootcamp at Eleven Fifty Academy. When i'm not writing code, you can find me hiking, running, thrift shopping, watching a bad horror movie, or exploring a new town with my camera.


Projects


Vagabond Travel App

An app where users can share interesting landmarks they find during road trips. Users can sign up and track the states and landmarks they have visited while checking out where everyone else has been.

Graffiti Finder App

An app that allows users to upload an image and location of cool street art they find. Anyone can come to the site if they want to share and find new art.

Studio Ghibli API

An app that fetches from the Studio Ghibli API to display a random movie. Removing the stress of deciding which film to see next.

Film Photography

My Instagram portfolio of my personal photography. I develop my own film and produce my own prints in my personal darkroom. My work has been displayed and sold at several coffee shops and art centers around Indianapolis.
